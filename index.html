Who We Are

Infinity is the student-run mathematics club of IISER Mohali. We are a community of math enthusiasts, problem solvers, and abstract thinkers. Our mission is to foster a love for mathematics beyond the classroom through engaging talks, challenging problem-solving sessions, and interactive events.

From game theory nights to deep dives into algebraic geometry, we explore it all. Whether you are a seasoned math major or just curious about the patterns of the universe, there is a place for you here.
