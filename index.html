01. Who We Are

Infinity is more than just a club; it's a collective of curious minds. Located at the heart of IISER Mohali, we are dedicated to fostering a culture of mathematical inquiry outside the traditional classroom setting.

Whether analyzing the probabilities in Game Theory, debating the foundations of Logic, or visualizing Topology, we believe mathematics is a creative pursuit. Our community is open to undergraduates, majors, and anyone fascinated by the language of the universe.
